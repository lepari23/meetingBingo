Welcome to Buzzword Bingo

Play a Game


How does this work?
  • Pick a list. Built-ins + your own.
  • Select a board size (3x3 - 7x7). Too few words? Size is refused.
  • Bonus square = centre “FREE” space on odd boards.
  • Click words as they're said to strike them.
  • Complete a row, column, or diagonal → Bingo!
  • Reset keeps the layout, New Board reshuffles.
  • Freeze locks a winning board so you can show proof.
  • Custom lists live only in localStorage. Export JSON to share.
  • Disclaimer: It's just for fun; play at your own risk.

The identity of Bingo is formally ambiguous, and it is occasionally suggested that it is the name of the farmer.
